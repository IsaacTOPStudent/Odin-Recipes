Hamburguer

Description

The hamburger is one of the most famous meals of all. And with fair reason is a meal that satisfies.

It is a meal that contains many ingredients and can be prepared in many ways.

Ingredients

  • 500g of ground beef

  • 4 hamburger buns

  • 8 strips of bacon

  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese (optional)

  • Lettuce leaves

  • Tomato slices

  • Red onion slices (optional)

  • Sauce of your choice

  • salt or pepper or both

Preparation steps

  1. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium-high heat.

  2. Form four hamburger patties with the ground beef, ensuring they are evenly thick so they cook evenly.

  3. Cook the hamburger patties on the grill or skillet for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked to your liking.

    If you choose to add cheddar cheese, place a slice on each patty a few minutes before removing them from the heat, allowing it to melt.

  4. While the hamburgers are cooking, fry the bacon strips in a separate skillet until crispy.

  5. Once the hamburgers are done, remove them from the heat and let them rest for a few minutes.

  6. Meanwhile, lightly toast the hamburger buns on the grill or skillet

  7. To assemble the burger, place a hamburger patty on the bottom half of each bun, then add the bacon,

    lettuce leaves, tomato slices, and onion slices, and finally the sauce of your choice. Top with the other half of the bun.

  8. ENJOY
