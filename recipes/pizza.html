Pizza

Description

Pizza is one of the most popular foods of recent times, a classic to have a good time with friends.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough

  • 100g Mozzarella

  • Tomato sauce

  • Vegetables to taste

Preparation steps

  1. Initially you must grease a mold that you can put in the oven safely.

  2. Then, flatten the pizza dough on the pan. If you have a roller it will be easier for you.

  3. For the pizza base, spread tomato sauce evenly. According to your taste you can apply the amount you want.

  4. Then spread the grated mozzarella cheese over the entire surface. If you want to add more it's ok.

  5. Customize your pizza with your favorite ingredients.

  6. eat your oven to 350Â°C and bake for 20 minutes.

  7. When it's ready, carefully take it out, let it cool slightly and enjoy.
